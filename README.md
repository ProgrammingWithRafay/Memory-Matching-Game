Memory Matching Game
A 2-player memory card matching game built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.
About
This is a browser-based card matching game where 2 players take turns flipping cards on a 4x4 board. The goal is to find all 6 matching pairs. The player who finds the most pairs wins. The game also tests your memory because when a pair is not found the cards flip back over, so you have to remember where each card was to use that information on your next turn.
How to Play

There are 12 cards placed face down on the board
Players take turns picking 2 cards
If the 2 cards match, that player gets 1 point and gets to go again
If the 2 cards don't match, they flip back over and it becomes the other player's turn
Pay attention even when it's not your turn! You can remember where cards are and use that information when it's your turn
The game ends when all 6 pairs are found
The player with the most points wins

Features

2 player turn based gameplay
12 cards with 6 emoji pairs
Score tracking for both players
Active player highlight so you always know whose turn it is
Cards stay visible for a moment after a failed match so players can memorize positions
Win screen showing final scores and the winner
New game button to restart anytime
